PHASE III: THE SLANG

What is "Ain't"?

In movies and games, you will hear the word AIN'T.
It is a very common informal word (slang) for the negative Verb To Be.

Am not

Ain't

"I ain't a hero."

Is not

Ain't

"He ain't here."

Are not

Ain't

"They ain't coming."

TRANSLATE THE SLANG

Connect the formal English to the informal "Ain't".

PHASE IV: THE ROUTINE

Present Simple

We use the Present Simple to talk about facts, habits, and routines.
A Porter's life is a routine.

I / You / We / They

"I walk every day."

"They deliver cargo."

He / She / It (+s)

"He walks every day."

"She carries the box."
